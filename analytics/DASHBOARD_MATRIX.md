## 🎨 **CREATIVE PAX DATASET USAGE**

### **🌍 Global Level - PAX Innovation**
- **PAX Flow Networks**: Visualize passenger flow patterns between countries
- **PAX Density Heatmaps**: Show passenger concentration by geographic regions
- **PAX Revenue Correlation**: Interactive scatter plots showing PAX volume vs revenue efficiency
- **PAX Growth Trends**: Time-series analysis of passenger volume changes
- **PAX Market Share**: Pie charts showing PAX distribution across countries

### **🌍 Country Level - PAX Innovation**
- **PAX Hub Analysis**: Identify which airports are true passenger hubs vs revenue hubs
- **PAX Efficiency Metrics**: Revenue per PAX vs PAX volume analysis
- **PAX Geographic Distribution**: How PAX is distributed within each country
- **PAX Performance Quadrants**: PAX volume vs revenue efficiency positioning
- **PAX Opportunity Sizing**: Identify airports with high PAX but low revenue

### **✈️ Airport Level - PAX Innovation**
- **PAX Revenue Attribution**: How much revenue each passenger generates
- **PAX Efficiency Benchmarking**: Compare PAX-to-revenue ratios with peers
- **PAX Growth Projections**: Predictive models based on PAX trends
- **PAX Market Positioning**: Where this airport stands in PAX vs revenue matrix
- **PAX Optimization Analysis**: How to improve revenue per PAX

### **🎨 Creative PAX Visualizations**
- **PAX Flow Sankey Diagrams**: Show passenger flow from countries to airports
- **PAX Bubble Maps**: Airports sized by PAX, colored by revenue efficiency
- **PAX Performance Quadrants**: Four-quadrant analysis (High PAX/High Revenue, etc.)
- **PAX Correlation Heatmaps**: Show relationships between PAX and other metrics
- **PAX Trend Lines**: Time-series visualization of passenger volume changes
